Founder & Lead Teacher

Pete Bromfield

Director of Studies & Enrichment at an IB Continuum School in Europe · Former Head of Maths · Former Head of Sixth Form (two schools) · Former IB Diploma Programme Coordinator · Practising IB Internal Assessment examiner and Paper examiner — over a decade of leadership across the IB continuum.

Pete is an experienced IB Diploma Programme mathematics teacher who has coached students across every DP maths course — SL AI, SL AA, HL AI and HL AA — through their final exams. His approach blends rigorous exam preparation with the personalised diagnostic work students need to move from a 5 to a 7.

Alongside classroom teaching, Pete provides one-to-one Internal Assessment support and hosts the online learning engines you'll find on this site. Every practice question, mark scheme and worked answer is written by him or reviewed line-by-line before it reaches you.

CBSE experience: Pete has served on a Council of International Schools (CIS) inspection team at a CBSE / A Level school, giving him first-hand insight into the CBSE mathematics curriculum, assessment demands and how it maps onto international programmes.
